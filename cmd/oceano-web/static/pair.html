Pairing Wizard

Prepare the RM4 Mini, then enter its IP address to start pairing.

  1. Plug the Broadlink RM4 Mini into power.
  2. Open the Broadlink app on your phone and connect it to your Wi-Fi network (first-time setup only).
  3. Once connected, note the device's IP address from your router's DHCP table or the app.
  4. Enter the IP below and click Start Pairing.
Must be reachable from the Raspberry Pi on the same network.
